Technical Architecture

Data Encoding

Network telemetry is normalized and encoded into quantum states using amplitude encoding techniques. Each feature maps to rotation angles in quantum gates.

Model Training

Hybrid QNN uses Quantum Natural Gradient descent. QSVM employs quantum kernel estimation. Quantum Autoencoder applies variational quantum eigensolver approach.

Measurement

Quantum states are measured multiple times (shots) to obtain probability distributions. Classical post-processing determines final anomaly classifications.
